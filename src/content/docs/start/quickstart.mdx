This is the 60-second tour. It assumes you've [installed Prevail](/start/installation/).

## Boot the cockpit

```bash
prevail demo     # safe synthetic vault — explore first
prevail          # use your own vault
```

You land in the cockpit:

```

│  domains            │  [chat] · state · quick start · prompts · skills    │
```

- **Left sidebar:** every life domain, plus every connected app below it.
- **Right workspace:** the active domain, with a consistent tab strip — `chat · state · quick start · prompts · skills`.
- **Bottom:** the ConfigBar carrying every per-turn dial.

## Get around

<Steps>

1. **Move between domains** with `↑` / `↓`.

2. **Swap focus to the apps list** (and back) with `s`.

3. **Edit the active markdown tab** in your `$EDITOR` with `e`.

4. **Start a slash command** by typing `/`. `/help` lists every one.

5. **Quit** with `q`.

</Steps>

Full list: [Keyboard shortcuts](/cockpit/keyboard/).

## Ask your first questions

Inside the chat pane, type:

```text
should I sell or rent the condo?
```

That goes to your single active CLI. To get the whole council instead:

```text
/council should I sell or rent the condo?
```

Prevail fans the question to every available engine in parallel, then a chair model synthesizes one verdict — with a panel showing exactly where the panelists disagreed.

```text
/framework bluf      switch the answer's structure to Bottom Line Up Front
/web on               allow this turn to reach the internet
/distill              turn this whole conversation into a reusable skill
```

<Aside type="tip">
You don't have to type `/council` every time. Toggle **Council ● ON** in the ConfigBar and *every* question fans out until you toggle it off.
</Aside>

## Save what you learn

Two things happen automatically as you work:

- Every turn writes a one-line summary to `<domain>/_log/YYYY-MM-DD.md` — your vault keeps its own decision log.
- `/distill` synthesizes the current conversation into a `SKILL.md` draft under `<domain>/skills/`, so a good workflow becomes a one-click action next time.

→ [Logs & journal](/vault/logs-journal/) · [Skills](/vault/skills/)

## Put it on your phone

```bash
prevail telegram setup <bot-token>     # token from @BotFather
prevail telegram add-user <chat-id>    # mandatory allowlist
prevail daemon --telegram              # poll Telegram + tick briefings
```

Now `/council`, `/domain wealth`, and `/framework bluf` all work from anywhere.

→ [Telegram & your phone](/advanced/telegram/)
